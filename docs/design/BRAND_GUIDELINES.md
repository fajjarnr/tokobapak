# TokoBapak Brand Guidelines

> **Version 1.0** | January 2026

---

## 1. Brand Identity

### Our Philosophy
**TokoBapak** is more than just a marketplace; it's a bridge connecting local sellers with millions of Indonesian families. The name "Bapak" (Father) evokes feelings of **trust, reliability, wisdom, and protection**.

### Voice & Tone
- **Trustworthy**: We speak with authority but not arrogance.
- **Friendly & Local**: We use language that feels like a neighbor ("Tetangga") talking to another.
- **Helpful**: We are solution-oriented.

---

## 2. Logo

### The Concept
The logo combines a modern shopping bag/cart element with a subtle, stylized "Mustache" or "Tie" motif (representing the "Bapak" figure) to create a unique, memorable icon that feels warm yet professional.

### Usage
- **Primary Logo**: Full icon + text. Used for headers, splash screens, and marketing materials.
- **Mark Only**: Icon only. Used for favicons, avatars, and app icons.
- **Clearspace**: Always maintain clear space equal to the height of the "B" in the wordmark around the logo.

---

## 3. Color Palette

We use a color palette that reflects **trust (Blue)**, **energy (Orange)**, and **stability (Slate/Gray)**.

### Primary Colors

| Color Name | Hex Code | Tailwind Class | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Bapak Blue** | `#0F172A` | `bg-slate-900` | Primary brand color, headers, text. Represents stability. |

| **Warm Orange** | `#F97316` | `bg-orange-500` | CTAs, sale badges, notifications. Represents energy & warmth. |

---

## 4. Typography

We use **Inter** for its excellent readability and modern aesthetic across all devices.

### Font Family
**Inter** (Google Fonts)

### Scale (Tailwind)
- **H1**: `text-4xl font-bold tracking-tight` (Page Titles)
- **H2**: `text-3xl font-semibold tracking-tight` (Section Headers)
- **H3**: `text-2xl font-semibold` (Card Groups)
- **Body**: `text-base font-normal text-slate-600` (Paragraphs)
- **Small**: `text-sm font-medium text-slate-500` (Captions)

---

## 5. UI Elements (shadcn/ui spec)

### Buttons
- **Primary**: `bg-slate-900 text-white hover:bg-slate-800`
- **Secondary**: `bg-white text-slate-900 border border-slate-200 hover:bg-slate-100`
- **Destructive**: `bg-red-500 text-white hover:bg-red-600`

### Cards
Clean, white background with subtle shadow (`shadow-sm`) and rounded corners (`rounded-xl`).
